Certainly! Think of it as the difference between having a teacher and exploring on your own:

  • Supervised Learning: You provide the AI with "labeled" data. For your Project Alpha dataset, this means telling the AI "this data point is a success" and "this is a failure." It learns the pattern to predict future outcomes.
  • Unsupervised Learning: You give the AI raw data without any labels. It looks for hidden structures, grouping your users into segments based on behavior patterns you might not have noticed yet.

For your current project, unsupervised learning would be perfect for identifying unique user personas before we start training a predictive model.
